# aruaru-llm Research

aruaru-llm is a research-oriented local LLM layer.

## Research Themes

- open-source local LLM integration
- model routing
- compression
- folding-style optimization concepts
- pseudo-quantum-inspired optimization concepts
- multiple GPU utilization
- aruaru-llm + external AI hybrid use

## Accuracy Policy

aruaru-llm must separate:

- official information
- measured benchmark results
- estimates
- hypotheses
- unverified research ideas

Unverified performance claims must never be displayed as confirmed facts.

## Practical Direction

For ordinary users and low-spec PCs, aruaru-llm should usually assist external AI rather than replace high-end paid AI completely.
